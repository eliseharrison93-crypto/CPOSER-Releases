# CPOSER

**CPOSER** automatically switches your standing idle pose based on the active Glamourer design.

## Setup

1. Open CPOSER with `/cposer`.
2. Click **Scan Glamourer**.
3. Assign `Idle 1` through `Idle 7` to your designs.
4. CPOSER automatically changes your pose when a linked Glamourer design becomes active.

## Penumbra Idle Mods

Configure your idle-animation mods under **Associated Mods** in Glamourer:

- Enable **State** for mods that should activate.
- Disable **State** for mods that should deactivate.
- Use a base design to reset outfit.
